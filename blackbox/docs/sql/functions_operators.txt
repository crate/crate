=======================
Functions and Operators
=======================

This chapter provides an overview of functions and operators that are permitted
for writing expressions in CrateDB. Operators are reserved symbols that are
primarily used in a :ref:`sql_dql_where_clause` to perform arithmetic operations
or comparisons.
